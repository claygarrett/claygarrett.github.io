Water Weirdos

I usually get some downtime when visiting the inlaws in Germany for the holidays. This year I tried my hand at some illustration. I started all of these with the eyeball and then just went where it took me. Where it took me was to the bottom of the ocean! Thinking of making a series for my son's room!

I sketched them all in ink my journal then inked them in Illustrator. Coloring and post processing in Photoshop.
